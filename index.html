Lavender Lane Baking Co. was founded with one purpose — to use pure ingredients to create healthy indulgences. As we like to say, our made-from-scratch baked goods are healthy…made delicious! If this all sounds a bit like baking magic, it is. But the magic is simple. Our bakers start by sourcing pure, organic, nutrient-packed ingredients from trusted purveyors. Then, we leave out the not-so-healthy things, like gluten, grains, refined sugars, soy, lactose, GMOs, trans fats, and artificial ingredients. We can even make your baked treats vegan, by special request. Lavender Lane Baking Co. is your source for healthy, delicious, handmade baked goods for (nearly) everyone to enjoy. They’re the perfect indulgences that you’ll want to share with friends, family, co-workers, clients and more!
