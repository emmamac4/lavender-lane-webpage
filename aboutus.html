Who We Are



What are a couple of moms (and friends) to do when they want to treat their families to delicious, healthy indulgences that even their daughters can enjoy — without worries for their food allergies, autoimmune disorders or other health concerns? For Christine and Sarah, the answer was to combine their backgrounds in baking, nutrition, mental health and entrepreneurial business development with their hunger for pure, wholesome indulgences. Together, they founded Lavender Lane Baking Co in 2018. …but not before an exhaustive search for the best purveyors of organic, pure, and all-natural ingredients. And, of course, months of endless baking and product testing — though none of their friends and family complained about the testing process! The result? A delectable selection of baked goods that won’t derail your pursuit of a more healthful, nutritious diet, while maintaining the ultimate flavor and texture of your all-time favorite treats. After all, at Lavender Lane, we understand that eating healthy doesn’t have to mean sacrificing taste or flavor. As Christine and Sarah like to say, Lavender Lane’s made-from-scratch baked goods are healthy…made delicious!
